The Snack Shack

The Snack Shack is our self-service cafe area. Here you will find fresh, filter coffee, a selection of teas as well as home made cookies and cakes. It makes for a comfortable space to eat lunch or take a break or meet up with friends.

If you are here for the morning, why not order a freshly-prepared lunch at reception? Orders must be made by 10:30am and food may be collected from 12:30pm.
