Social Distancing: Resources for Self Care and Wellbeing

These are testing times for all members of our community and it's made harder by the fact that we are unable to come together and support one another in the ways we are used to.

As and when we can, we will post some resources here that you may wish to download to help you look after yourself and pass the time. It may not be much but hopefully it might be of use to some of you.

Take care of yourselves and best wishes from all of us here at OSCP.

Story Sharing Ideas

Sharing our personal stories and histories has always brought people closer. Now that we have a bit of time, maybe we could reminisce together?

Perhaps you're fortunate enough to be social-distancing with family or friends, but how much of each other's histories do you know? Maybe you could discover something previously unknown about someone with whom you're in phone contact?
